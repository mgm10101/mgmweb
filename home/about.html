The MGM Academy Story

MGM Academy is an upcoming learning institution based in a fast-growing residential area in Malaa along Kangundo Road, just after Joska. A peaceful, serene environment surrounded by nature where children can learn in tranquility and discover the importance of conservation. The school is committed to the best interests of students, guided by our core values and professional ethics.

MGM Academy is built on the foundation of promoting the welfare of all children, nurturing their potential to allow them to freely express themselves and grow with confidence.

The school follows the Montessori teaching method for early years and graduates learners to the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC). Our teaching style emphasizes exposure to nature and learning through play. In the early years, children are especially open to learning from their environments, encouraging a sense of unity with the natural world.

Our Mission

To develop confident, independent young learners through an academic experience that ignites curiosity to unlock each child’s potential.

Our Vision

To provide quality childcare in a safe and secure environment; supporting children to acquire skills, competencies, and experiences that prepare them for their learning journey through school and life.

Values:

  • Safe and secure environment where everyone thrives
  • Discipline—where children learn honesty, respect, kindness, and togetherness
  • Excellence—Setting our own high standards and celebrating the achievements of others
  • Diversity—Embracing diverse aspects of each child and celebrating them

What We Offer

MGM Academy aims to make the community more environmentally conscious. Along with excelling in academic studies, we emphasize individual growth.

Environmental Activities

We prioritize environmental awareness by integrating it into the curriculum. Children learn about nature, preservation, and the importance of protecting the environment.

Language Activities

In preschool, children develop essential language skills: listening, writing, and articulation through interactive activities.

Math Activities

Our math activities focus on counting, sorting, adding, subtracting, and sequencing through fun games and creative projects.

Creative Activities

We encourage children to express themselves artistically through painting, drawing, designing, and other creative outlets.

Science Activities

Children explore the wonders of science through hands-on experiments about measurements, magnets, plants, and more.

Motor Skills Development

Outdoor and indoor games help children develop motor skills like throwing, catching, and balancing while enjoying fresh air.
