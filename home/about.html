Welcome to MGM Academy

MGM Academy is an upcoming learning institution based in a fast-growing residential area in Malaa along Kangundo Road, just after Joska. A peaceful, serene environment surrounded by nature where children can learn in tranquility and discover the importance of conservation.

The school is committed to the best interests of students, guided by our core values and professional ethics. MGM Academy is built on the foundation of promoting the welfare of all children, nurturing their potential to allow them to freely express themselves and grow with confidence.

Our Teaching Philosophy

Blending Montessori with Competency-Based Education

Montessori Method

The school follows the Montessori teaching method for early years, emphasizing self-directed activity, hands-on learning, and collaborative play. Children make creative choices in their learning while the classroom and teacher offer age-appropriate activities to guide the process.

Competency-Based Curriculum

We graduate learners to the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), focusing on developing skills and competencies rather than just content knowledge. Our teaching style emphasizes exposure to nature and learning through play.

Nature-Based Learning

In the early years, children are especially open to learning from their environments, encouraging a sense of unity with the natural world. Our campus provides the perfect setting for this holistic approach to education.

What We Offer

Comprehensive learning experiences for holistic development

MGM Academy aims to make the community more environmentally conscious. Along with excelling in academic studies, we emphasize individual growth through diverse activities.

Environmental Activities

We prioritize environmental awareness by integrating it into the curriculum. Children learn about nature, preservation, and the importance of protecting the environment.

Language Activities

In preschool, children develop essential language skills: listening, writing, and articulation through interactive activities and storytelling.

Math Activities

Our math activities focus on counting, sorting, adding, subtracting, and sequencing through fun games and creative projects.

Creative Activities

We encourage children to express themselves artistically through painting, drawing, designing, and other creative outlets.

Science Activities

Children explore the wonders of science through hands-on experiments about measurements, magnets, plants, and more.

Motor Skills Development

Outdoor and indoor games help children develop motor skills like throwing, catching, and balancing while enjoying fresh air.

Music & Movement

Through songs, dance, and rhythm activities, children develop coordination, listening skills, and creative expression.

Social Development

Group activities and collaborative projects help children develop social skills, empathy, and teamwork abilities.
