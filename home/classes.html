Our Age-Based Learning Programs

At MGM Academy, we offer carefully structured programs tailored to each developmental stage. Our classrooms are designed to provide a nurturing environment where children can explore, discover, and grow at their own pace.

Peach Class: Age 1-3 Years (Crèche)

Our highly trained teachers help your child with key milestones of this important developmental stage in a caring environment that provides safety to have the freedom to explore within boundaries. There is individual attention, enrichment, and praise.

Releasing your child to us must be difficult, therefore we constantly communicate with you about all the important moments of his/her day such as diapering, eating, sleeping and above all the exciting new learning experiences he/she has had.

Individual attention Safe exploration environment Constant parent communication Developmental milestone focus

Orange Class: Age 4+ Years (PP1)

Our program combines kindergarten readiness activities. It is designed to meet the childcare and learning needs of the 5-year-old and prepare them for the transition. The curriculum focuses on maths, writing, science, environmental and social development in a fun appropriate program.

We do not forget plenty of outdoor activities where your child will also learn while exercising their body as well as his/her mind. Our approach balances structured learning with creative play.

Kindergarten readiness Math & writing foundations Outdoor learning activities Social development focus

Lavender Class: Age 5+ Years (PP2)

The curriculum focuses on writing, maths, science and social development. We make sure that all children receive the attention needed to confidently tackle the learning opportunities that will prepare them for Junior Primary.

This stage emphasizes building strong academic foundations while continuing to develop social skills and independence in a supportive learning environment.

Writing skill development Math & science concepts Junior primary preparation Confidence building

Grade 1 - Grade 3

At MGM we are conscious of the developmental stages of the child and teach in tandem with this. Students that graduate into grade 1 are usually at age 6+, this is the stage where children learn through feeling. With a good foundation in their early years they start to make sense of their surroundings and absorb what they are taught like sponges.

This is when they improve on their reading skills, learn to put their thoughts and feelings into place. Our teaching is learner-centred and therefore we start our day with story time from both the children and the teachers. Through story time we inspire the children and allow them to create their own story using the environment as their guide or folk tales. The story of the day is then incorporated into the learning activities of maths, language, sciences etc. We make learning fun and acceptable for the students.

Story-based learning approach Reading skill enhancement Environmental integration Holistic skill development
