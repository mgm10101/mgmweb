Extra Curricular Activities

At MGM Academy, we believe learning should not be confined to the classroom. We explore all areas of education to nurture your child to be the best they can be: both in and out of the classroom.

Our extracurricular programs are designed to develop physical skills, creativity, social awareness, and a sense of adventure in every child.

Below are some of our exciting activities:

Swimming

The students are taken weekly for swimming, starting at the daycare stage. They begin by being introduced to the water slowly, but by the end of the term, they are confident and ready for full swimming lessons. Water safety and basic strokes are taught by certified instructors in a safe, supervised environment.

Sports Day

We spend loads of time playing and exercising! Our Sports Day brings healthy competition to our community of parents, staff, and children. Events include relay races, obstacle courses, ball games, and fun team challenges that promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Field Trips

MGM Academy believes in taking students out of their environment to explore different places. These trips open their minds to new opportunities and provide valuable insight into our country. Destinations include museums, farms, nature reserves, historical sites, and science centers.

Talent Show

During the term, students are introduced to music, dance, and acting. These skills are showcased in our annual talent show where parents and family can see the amazing talents of their children. This event builds confidence, creativity, and performance skills in a supportive environment.
