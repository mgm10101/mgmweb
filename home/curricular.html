Extra Curricular Activities

At MGM Academy we believe learning should not be confined to the classroom.

Therefore we explore all areas of education to nurture your child to be the best they can be. In and out of the class.

Below are some of our activities

Swimming

The students are taken weekly for swimming and this starts at the daycare stage. They start by being introduced to the water slowly, however, towards the end of the term, they are confident and ready for the swimming lessons.

Sports Day

We spend loads of our time playing and exercising. How about some healthy competition with our community of parents, staff, and children?

Field Trips

MGM academy believes that taking students out of their environment to different places to explore this opens up their minds to the opportunities that are available to them and gives them valuable insight into our country.

Talent Show

During the term, the students are introduced to music, dance, and acting, which is showcased at the end of the year where parents and family see what talented children they have.
